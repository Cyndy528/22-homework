My Passion Project

One passion project I would like to one day complete would be to allow for people to annotate and comment on current legislation under consideration. It would, in my ideal world, allow for things like loopholes, riders and pork to be fished out by the crowd. Also, people could comment and submit updates to various aspects of the legistlation ala github push/pull requests. anything that allows for better government between the people and their elected representatives is worthwhile to me. With the skills I will learn in WDI I will implement this idea.
